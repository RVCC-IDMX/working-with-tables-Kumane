DJ Kev's 9 Song Review

Hey what's up guys! DJ Kev here. I've posted a table below of some songs I'll be playing on RVCC Internet Radio! Be sure to check out the full list!

Artist Song Title Release Year Country of Origin Music Style Rating and Comments
Quest Master The Divine Rays of the Sunrise Eclipse 2017 Australia Dungeon Synth 4 Stars : Could use more dungeon and less stereotypical fantasy
Pentakill Cull 2017 United States of America Heavy Metal 5 Stars: S-Tier Heavy Metal that is executed perfectly, drawing inspiration from classics while still being innovative.
Arcana A Cage 2017 Sweden Neoclassical Darkwave 4 Stars: The soft vocals and chilling harmonies make this song transformative, making you feel like a spirit of Swedish folklore.
Barnacle Boi Still. 2019 United States of America Phonk 3 Stars: Repetitive. Very reptitive melodies, but the distorted vocals keep the track interesting.
MISOGI, BONES Eyesclosed (feat.BONES) 2014 United States of America Emo Rap 3.5 Stars: The instrumentation of the track is excellent, but the lyrical content is lacking in originality.
Bring Me The Horizon Oh No 2015 United States of America Pop Rock 5 Stars: The juxtaposition of upbeat melodies contrasted by depressing lyrics makes this song a capitvating listen.
Pleasurekraft Troller 2015 United States of America Cosmic Techno 4 Stars: An interesting song only held back by it's repetitive drum loop, though that is expected of the genre. Pleasurekraft makes a lot of interesting sounds to help layer the track.
Daisuke Ishiwatari, Naoki Hashimoto HEAVY DAY 2015 Japan Heavy Metal Soundtrack 5 Stars + a few more: An absolutely incredible song that is a masterclass in video game music. Heavy, melodic, symphonic. The works.
Bastille Pompeii 2013 United Kingdom Pop 4.5 Stars: It's hard to believe this song is 11 years old already. It's a unique, cinematic, and melancholic pop classic.
Escape the Fate My Apocalypse 2006 United States of America Metalcore 4 Stars: The song is hardcore but a the sound is a bit dated. It's hard to separate the artist from their very many controversies as well.
